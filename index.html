DigitalSalon

DigitalSalon is a group of technologists who meet in Beacon, NY.

Once a week, on a Tuesday night, you can find us in the warm Bank Square Coffeehouse at the start of Main St.

At other times, you can chat with us online.

We often spend our time together making things. You can browse some of our software projects on github.

Anyone with a keen interest to learn and to teach is welcome.
